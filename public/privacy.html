Privacy Policy for Mandala Day

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Mandala Day (“the app”) is a meditation and mindfulness application published by the Mandala Day team. This policy explains what information the app handles, where it is stored, and what rights you have.

Summary

  • The app does not require an account.
  • The app does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal information.
  • All practice data, schedules, and preferences are stored only on your device.
  • The app does not track your location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera.
  • The web version of Mandala Day uses Google Analytics for anonymous aggregate traffic statistics. The mobile app does not.

Information the app handles

Stored only on your device

The following information is saved locally on your device using the operating system’s standard secure storage and never leaves it:

  • Your meditation schedule (session times, which sessions are enabled, quiet hours)
  • Your app settings (notification preferences, onboarding status)
  • Your session history for the past 30 days (completed, skipped, or snoozed sessions)
  • An internal event log used for in-app features (capped and rotated automatically)

You can remove all of this data at any time by uninstalling the app or by using your device’s “Clear app data” option.

Notifications

With your permission, the app schedules local reminders for your meditation sessions. These notifications are generated and delivered entirely on your device. Mandala Day does not operate notification servers and does not send push notifications from the internet.

Audio playback

The app plays bundled meditation audio files. Audio is played locally; nothing is streamed from our servers, and no audio from your device (voice, microphone) is recorded or transmitted. The app explicitly blocks microphone access.

Background activity

To keep your meditation audio playing when the screen is off, and to deliver on-time reminders, the app uses standard Android and iOS background-audio and scheduled-notification capabilities. No data is sent off the device as part of this activity.

Information the web version handles

If you use Mandala Day in a web browser at the Mandala Day website, Google Analytics records anonymous, aggregate information about how the site is used (e.g., which pages are viewed, approximate geographic region, browser type). This information is used only to understand overall usage and is not tied to your identity. You can opt out using any standard “Do Not Track” or analytics-blocking browser setting or extension. The mobile app (iOS and Android) does not include Google Analytics or any other analytics SDK.

Information the app does not collect

Mandala Day does not:

  • Create user accounts or require login
  • Collect your name, email address, phone number, or payment information
  • Access your contacts, calendar, photos, location, microphone, or camera
  • Track your activity across other apps or websites
  • Sell, rent, or share your information with advertisers
  • Include third-party advertising SDKs

Children’s privacy

Mandala Day is appropriate for general audiences and does not knowingly collect information from children. Because the app collects no personal information from anyone, it does not collect personal information from children under 13 (or under 16 in applicable jurisdictions).

Your rights

Because your data is stored only on your device, you are in direct control of it:

  • Access: Your data is visible within the app itself.
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the app or clearing its storage through your device settings removes all locally stored data.
  • Portability: The app does not currently offer an export feature. Contact us if you would like one.

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, California, or another jurisdiction with applicable privacy laws, the rights described above satisfy the typical access and deletion rights under those laws for the limited on-device data the app stores.

Security

Data is stored using the standard secure storage APIs provided by iOS (Keychain-backed UserDefaults) and Android (internal app storage). Because no data is transmitted to a server operated by us, there is no server-side data to secure.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new “Last updated” date. Material changes will be noted in the app release notes.
